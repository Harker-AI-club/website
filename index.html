Who We Are

Harker Artificial Intelligence Club

Since our founding in 2017, our mission has been to inspire and educate Harker students in the field of artificial intelligence. Through student-led events, we provide the knowledge and resources necessary for students to build their own AI models and apply them in the real world.

Join our Discord

Our Initiatives

Please take a look at our iniatives for the 2022-23 year

AI Workshops

Learn about the theory behind AI models through detailed presentations and program AI models of your own. We provide training for all programming levels, from Python to Pandas.

Guest Speakers

Learn from the experiences of AI professors, researchers, and industry professionals working at the cutting edge of the field. In the past, we've brought in speakers from Stanford University, Google, and Carnegie Mellon University.

AI Journal

Submit articles, research papers, or any work related to AI to be featured in our journal! More details will be sent out soon.
